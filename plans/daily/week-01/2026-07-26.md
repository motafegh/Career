# Deliverable R3 — Evaluator gate judgment

The evaluator uses these definitions.

## Pass

- every Week 1 hard-gate item exists and passes;
- actual evidence supports the claims;
- no unresolved gate-critical defect remains;
- state and ownership limits are explicit.

## Partial

- substantial delivery exists;
- one or more required items are incomplete;
- no single external blocker fully prevented work;
- Week 2 cannot begin safely without a bounded carry item.

## Blocked

- a specific technical/environment blocker prevented the gate;
- evidence and attempted diagnosis are preserved;
- the blocker is still active;
- unrelated work did not replace it.

## Failed

- required work was not delivered and no sufficient technical/capacity explanation exists;
- or scope diversion/new-project behavior displaced the plan;
- or evidence is too weak to validate claimed completion.

Hours alone do not pass the gate. A technically passed gate with fewer hours remains a passed delivery and triggers workload-density review rather than forced busywork.
